The Best Movie According to Harvey

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Inside Out 2

This sequel to the beloved 2015 animated film “Inside Out” continues to explore the complex emotions of Riley as she navigates her teenage years. The story delves deeper into the emotional and psychological challenges of adolescence, introducing new emotions alongside the familiar ones from the first film. The movie promises a heartfelt and humorous journey through Riley’s evolving inner world.

Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire

This latest installment in the MonsterVerse franchise follows the epic showdown between Godzilla and Kong, leading to a new chapter where both monsters must unite against a common, more formidable enemy. The film promises massive, spectacular battles and explores the dynamics between these two titanic creatures.

A Quiet Place: Day One

The third film in the “A Quiet Place” series takes the audience back to the first days of the alien invasion, showcasing the initial chaos and horror as society collapses under the threat of the sound-sensitive creatures. This prequel explores new characters and perspectives, adding depth to the already intense franchise.
